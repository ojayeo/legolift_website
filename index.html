About LegoLift

LegoLift is a social enterprise and outreach project that aims to uplift low-income undergraduate students in Malaysia by helping them generate side income and develop marketable skills. Through LEGO-building workshops, these students teach primary school children creative and technical skills, enhancing both their own employability and the students STEM knowledge. This initiative is supported by both YLA & Sasbadi Malaysia and aligns with our commitment to empowering the next generation.
